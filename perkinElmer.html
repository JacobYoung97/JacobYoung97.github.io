Employer Information

PerkinElmer is an American company founded in 1937. They focus in the areas of human and environmental health. This includes environmental analysis, food and consumer product safety, and life science research to name a few. The software, medical imaging components, instruments, and consumables help many end markets. Some of these markets are Animal Health, Food & Agriculture, and Therapeutic Areas. Their offices are located all over the world; including (but not limited to) the United States, Canada (30 minutes east of Waterloo and an hour west of Toronto), France, South Africa, Japan, Australia, and Brazil.
